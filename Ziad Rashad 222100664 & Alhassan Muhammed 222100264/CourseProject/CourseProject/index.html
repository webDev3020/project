Our Top Exercises

Push-Ups

Push-ups are a great bodyweight exercise to strengthen your chest, shoulders, and triceps. Perform them regularly to build upper body strength and endurance.

squats

Squats help to build strength in your legs, glutes, and core. Incorporate squats into your routine to improve lower body strength and mobility.

Plank

The plank is a fantastic exercise for strengthening the core. It's a simple but effective move for improving posture and overall stability.
